Cricket in india

How it got started

Cricket was introduced to India by British sailors in the 18th century, and the ﬁrst cricket club was established in 1792. India's national cricket team played its ﬁrst Test match on 25 June 1932 at Lord's, becoming the sixth team to be granted test cricket status
